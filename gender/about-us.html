Domestic violence, particularly gender-based violence against young girls and women, remains a pressing social challenge. We aim to create a dynamic, interactive platform to educate families and communities on non-violent approach to settling differences and promote healthier non violence relationships.

Gender-based violence (GBV) is one of the most silent yet devastating crises facing our communities today. Often hidden behind closed doors, it leaves scars that run deeper than the eye can see—scars on individuals, families, and entire societies. From subtle signs of emotional abuse to severe physical harm, GBV takes many forms, fueled by inequality, harmful cultural norms, and silence

Understanding the causes, recognizing the signs, and knowing the devastating effects is the first step toward change. But beyond awareness lies action: prevention strategies, community-driven solutions, and support systems that empower survivors and hold perpetrators accountable. This blog sheds light on GBV in all its complexity—its signs, causes, effects, and most importantly, the paths we can take together to prevent it and support those affected.
