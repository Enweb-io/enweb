Welcome to Genesis Gospel Audio application. Gospel music is a genre that spreads Christian beliefs through songs of worship, praise and thanks giving. Gospel music is truly a fulfilling spirtual experience for all believers. We find peace and joy in gospel music because the person of the Holy Spirit abides with those who enjoy God’s music. Much is written about God and much is written about Jesus, God’s son. Not much is written nor is known about the person of the Holy Spirit. God left his spirit on this earth to comfort, bless and call to himself his creation. We have a deep and abiding relationship with our Lord.
As we fill our minds and hearts with gospel music that allows us to worship and pray to our Creator, he comes close to us in an intimate way so that we can know him and feel his deep love. So our Lord created music to enrich us. My encouragement to you is that your Soul and Spirit will prosper and your mind will be more peaceful as you listen to the music that nourishes your spiritual life.
Blessings to you.

MY INSPIRATION

In primary school a girl named Maria and I were appointed as prefects. The first time I ever read the bible in front of people was in my sixth grade. It was during the school assembly under the the zambian flying flag in front of the the whole school. I was nervous but once I oepned that small blue book I was the bearer of the good news. After that Maria delivered an outstanding performance leading the choir with a golden beautiful voice. It was thrilling, exiting and very spiritual. I recall one lady teacher say that I was fit to be a prefect. I got exposed to songs of praise and worship in primary school and I enjoyed every moment of it.
Unfortunately Maria past away even before we got to the tenth grade. But I will never forget that wonderful spiritual experience I shared with her. In senior secondary at David Kaunda we formed a Gospel group named the praise machines. We met after prep either in the dining hall or at the hostels and we rehearsed, it was fun and very fulfilling. It was a combination of rap and ragga gospel music, it was loud. That is why we have a loud category on the application.
