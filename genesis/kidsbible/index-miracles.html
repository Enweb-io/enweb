MIRACLES PERFORMED BY JESUS

Michael

We will soon have a lot of Jesus stories. Something for you to look forward to but for now let us read about all the wonderful miracles performed by Jesus. The first question we should ask ourselves is why did Jesus perform miracles right in front of people? Jesus's answer to John tells us a lot about the purpose of His miracles: They are a sign of the prophecies fulfilled, a sign that Jesus is the long-awaited Messiah. They are a sign of His authority, His power, and His glory. And they are a sign of God's love for us, a sign that we can trust Him. Many believed in God after they witnessed the miracles. They are proof that with God nothing is impossible and that he is almighty and powerful. Here we look at the top miracles performed by Jesus through out his life on earth.

JESUS TURNED WATER INTO WINE

Michael

The first miracle performed by Jesus was to turn water into wine at a wedding. Jesus was told by his mother Mary that there was no wine at the wedding and he told her that it was not his time. Yet he went along and turned water into wine.

JESUS HEALED THE SICK

Michael

Jesus healed a lot of sick people in different places and at diferent times. That included women, men and children. Jesus loves the little children and he wants all of them to be his friends.

JESUS WALKED ON WATER

Michael

Jesus walked on water right in front of his disciples showing them that he indeed was the son of God sent to save humanity from the curse of sin

JESUS CALMED THE SEA STORM

Michael

Jesus commanded the sea to be still and calm and it obeyed as the storm disappeared and the waters were calm once again.

JESUS RESTORED THE SIGHT OF THE BLIND

Michael

Blind people are people who cannot see. Jesus came to the blind and restored their sight. At one point he picked cray from the ground spat on it and rubbed it on the eyes of the blind and his sight was restored.

JESUS RAISED LAUZARUS FROM THE DEAD

Michael

Lazurus was a young man who got sick and died when Jesus was away. When he got to the tomb a few days had passed. Jesus ordered the tomb to be opened and called out Lazarus by his name and he rose from the dead and came out.

JESUS HEALED A SICK WOMAN

Michael

A sick woman was healed after she touched the clothes that Jesus wore at the time. She had been sick for over 12 years and doctors could help her yet Jesus came and restored her health.

JESUS FED MULTITUDES WITH JUST 2 FISH AND 5 LOAVES OF BREAD

Michael

Jesus performed a very powerful miracle when he mutliplied food consisting of just two fish and five loaves of bread and fed a lot of people. Everyone ate and a lot of food that remained was collected and put in baskets to be eaten later.

JESUS HEALED A YOUNG GIRL

Michael

A man named Jairus came to Jesus and told him that his little daughter whom he loved so much was gravely ill. Jairus was scared that he would lose his daughter. But he was faithful enough to believe that Jesus would heal her. Jairus took Jesus to his little daughter and Jesus healed her.

JESUS DROVE AWAY EVIL SPIRITS

Michael

Evil spirits are bad spirits that work against God's plan for us. God wants us to be happy, loving and successful. Bad spirits want us to be sad, unhappy and failures. Therefore Jesus commanded evil spirits to leave God's people and hence drove them away.

DRAWING CHALLENGE

Now let us have some fun with Jesus and Lazarus in a drawing challenge. Take a plane paper and write your name on top and draw a picture of Jesus and Lazarus using a pencil, do not color the drawing. Label the picture Jesus and Lazarus, under the label write one thing that you learnt from the story of Jesus and Lazarus. Put it where you can see it and remember the story of Jesus and Lazarus and what you learnt from it. You can take a picture of the drawing and share it with us. If it is that good we will publish it. You can do many and choose the best. The drawing should fill the whole page so that we can see it.
