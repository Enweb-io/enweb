Welcome to Aunt Edina's Bible stories for kids. My name is Michael and I will be your host while you are using this fun Bible story application for kids. Those of us who were blessed enough to be in the presence of Aunt Edina know that she is radiant spirit now. She had one message for all her nieces, nephews, grand daughters and grand sons. She repeated it like a sweet melody that played over and over as she sang to the kids the song, "Jesus Loves The Little Children". Mandipa, Temwani and Chengelo know the song very well because she even sang it over the phone when she called to check on the kids. That is why she is always loved and forever in our hearts.
It was her intention to bring Jesus to the little ones. To bring the little ones into the presence of the Lord was a gesture born of a kind and loving heart. Because that was her nature and she was special in that way. She enjoyed singing for the little ones very very much because she loved them all, this kids Bible story picks up from where she left with intent to bring the little ones into the presence of God and our lord Jesus Christ, so that they are offered the divine protection and the word of God that they need nourish their growing faith.
We have a lot of fun Bible stories to share with the kids and we are excited to have you on board.Each story is accompanied by an audio version that you can play and listen to. Each story ends with an important lesson for the child. We also have a drawing challenge that will make the kids remember the stories and what they learnt from the tales. It is important to understand the intent and meaning of the story. That is the only way the word of God will take root and work in a child. We strongly recommend that the parents and guardians guide the little ones through the stories. Thank you very much and God Bless You.
