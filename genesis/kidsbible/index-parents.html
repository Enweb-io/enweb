BRING THE KIDS TO CHRIST

Aunt Edina

The Bible says raise your children in a way that honors God, with love, discipline, and instruction. This includes teaching children about Jesus, creating christain family traditions like family prayer and Bible study sessions, and serving others. By doing so you become a blessing to the world, to the kids and the kingdom of heaven.
Key points to remember:
Lead by example:
Be a living example of Christian values in your own actions and words.
Early exposure to scripture:
Introduce Bible stories and teachings appropriate for their age from a young age.
Prayer and worship:
Regularly pray together as a family and attend religious services.
Teach about right and wrong:
Explain the concept of sin and the importance of making good choices based on God's teachings.
Nurture a relationship with God:
Encourage your children to develop a personal relationship with God through prayer and reflection.
Godly discipline:
Discipline with love and understanding, using biblical principles as a guide.
Unconditional love:
Show your children consistent love and support, reflecting God's love for them.
Community involvement:
Participate in church activities and community service to foster a sense of belonging and responsibility. Kid poetry,bible quiz and are drama help kids remeber religious teachings and practices.
Teach children about Jesus.
Introduce children to the concept of sin and forgiveness through Jesus's sacrifice.
Teach children Scripture.
Model a godly life.
Create christian family traditions.
Make christian family time a priority.
Create biblical habits, such as nightly prayers and Bible sharing.
Serve others together Follow Jesus's example of serving others.
Pray with your children.
Pray for your children throughout the day.
Plead with God to draw them closer to himself and protect them from all the evil forces whose intent is to cause harm
Provide for their needs Create a stable and harmonious home and Provide for their needs.
Walk closely with God Lead by example and Nurture their spiritual growth.
Bible verses about children.
Ephesians 6:4, "Fathers, do not provoke your children to anger, but bring them up in the discipline and instruction of the Lord"
Proverbs 22:6, "Train up a child in the way he should go; even when he is old he will not depart from it".
Proverbs 29:15, "The rod and reproof give wisdom, but a child left to himself brings shame to his mother" .
To raise Godly children, focus on actively incorporating your faith into daily life by setting a Godly example, teaching them Bible stories and principles from a young age, spending quality time together in prayer and worship, and demonstrating love and discipline guided by Christian values; essentially, making your faith a central part of your parenting approach.
Nurture their spiritual lives.
Sunday school, kid's service and children's mass have a massive impact on the spirtual life of a child. They learn so much and as they grow up they will use the word of God to shape their characters. With that knowledge it is impossible for them to place themselves on the path that leads to destruction. Take your children to church and after church sit together to reflect and share the message on that day. Get them religious books and children's Bible. Do a family Bible quiz and ask them questions as well as correct them where they fail to get it right. That part which you play as a parent when you take your kids to church, is a gesture born of a kind and loving heart. It is also your christian duty to do so. It will also benefit your spiritual life as well because God will look with mercy and favor upon you because you have done a beautiful thing.
You should worry about the future of your children. When they are adults, they might go through trials and tribulations to whom will they turn to lighten their burden when all these years you have kept them away from the church and from Christ. Sometimes God does not offer a solution instead she provides us with the strength to carry our cross. From where will they get their strength if not from the living Christ and God the father. How will they put on their full armor of God when they have not known him? Wow will they resist the devil and evil temptation? God is begging you right now if you love your children take them to church. If you cannot them bring the church to them because you have the power to do so.
Blessings to you.

FAMILY CHRISTIAN TRADITIONS

Christian family traditions are events or special times dedicated to God and our Lod Jesus Christ. Activities may include fun with Bible story telling, religious drawing and paiting, drama, role play, Christian cartoons, reciting the lord's prayer, Bible study, praying together, Bible quiz and reading christain books and magazines. All these tradition can help establish a stronger family and allow kids to grow in a way that is righteous before the throne of God. Divine guidance and protection are realized through family christian traditions. It nourishes the faith of each family member because they unite in Christ to share the word of God. Families that pray together are stronger and more united and however high the tide is or deep the waters are they will be alright because they are strong together and God will look with mercy and favor upon them.
