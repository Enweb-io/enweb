1. ISOLATE ONE CUPCAKE

Quite ofen than regular we place the cupcakes in the box and take photos while we wait for client to pick them up. If your cupcakes have a wow factor they will look nice. But is is very important that you isolate one cupcake and take an upclose photo. This will bring out the details of your design and the cupcake photo will look beautiful on social media there by bringing more eyes to the product. For starter use a solid background so that all the attention falls on the cupcake. You can use a bed sheet or a colored cloth.
2. GOOD FOR CUPCAKE FLYERS

Solid backgrounds make it very for you to place text and promotion assets over the photo using any photo app. That way the design is attactive and neat. An excellent way to advertise your beautiful cupcakes. Try different colors like red, pink light, blue, peach and other colors that look good.
GET YOUR CUPCAKE FLYER 3. DEPTH OF FIELD

This is a photography effect that blurs the background of an image making the subject stand out. For a this you need an advanced phone that supports depth of field, iphone and other modern camera phone have this photo effect. This will create a beautiful photo your adverts.
4. INCREASE THE COUNT

Cupcakes look better out of the box because they are fully visible. From one isolated cupcake you can move to three and the whole half dozen hold.
5. USE A DARK THEME

A dark theme is comparable to solid backgroud and depth of field because it also allows the cupcakes to stand out with everything dark around it. This is not good for dark themed cupcakes like chocolate.
6. USE SPRINKLES

A cupcake with sprinkles scattered over the canvas gives it a nice unique presentation try it out and see how it looks
7. EXPERIEMENT WITH FRUIT AND PLANT

God's green earth is beautiful and if we take part of that green and create a nice cupcake image we will feel good about our product and market it easily.
8. NAKED CUPCAKE

She called requesting for a dozen hold of naked cupcakes and I got confused. I did not know what a naked cupcake is until the client explained that it is plain cupcake without cream or dressing. Therefore take photo of your naked cupcakes before you dress them up. Some clients like it that way they do not want their cup cakes to be dressed.
9. LIGHT IT UP

A tiny candle lite on top of the cupcake is a cool and interesting way for bakers to showcase their cupcakes.
10. CHOCOLATE CUPCAKES

Get creative with chocolate by using wooden surfaces sprinkled with particles of chocolate some berries and what ever you imagine. Visualize and bring out the scene exactly the way you see it. Get creative and make your product stand. You can use a large chopping board and a black cloth in the background.
11. BIRTH'S VIEW

Take a top shoot and it is ease to do this just make sure that the cupcakes are placed lower enough on a clean surface. This will highlight a full view of how the cupcakes look on top.
12. USE NATURAL LIGHT

Natural light gives a perfect image and this is because it is evenly distributed. This will require you to take the shot outside.
