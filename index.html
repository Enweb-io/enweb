DO NOT GET FRUSTRATED

Michael

Frustration occurs when you fail to get it right a few times and you do not know where the fault lies. You have followed the recipe to the letter, you have the right oven temperature and the right combination of ingredients yet it fails to bake and emotions take over. As a beginner you will run into such problems early on from time to time. It is normal and it is part of the learning process. But do not let it tame you. Getting around frustration is above preventing events that will lead to frustration. I know bakers that quit because they could not handle the pressure.
