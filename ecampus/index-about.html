Student Journalist
  • By Campus News

Student journalist means a student of a public or a private institution of higher education such as a College or University who gathers, compiles, writes, edits, photographs, records, or prepares information for dissemination in school-sponsored media. This allows the student journalist to prepare for the industry by taking on real life Journalism tasks while they are still learning.
Campus News is platform for student Journalists to test their research, report, writing and journalism skills. Campus News is non profit Open Project and participation is by choice. The project is purely implemented by Students.
Campus News is split into Academic Content and Campus Life style out class and usually told with Campus photos.
If you were an editor or one of the writers in your school Aunt Edina news blog, it would be a huge benefit for your career prospects.
Of course, journalism offers the possibility to reveal what lies in your heart. It works almost like blogging. Many students blog because they like to express their thoughts and highlight interesting topics.
Journalism involves various academic skills. Writing skills are indeed among the most crucial for any student.

Target Audience

This project is available to any Journalism student who has interest in sharing their stories, articles and news blogs. It is highly recommednded for final year students both at College and University level.
This project is a product of creative students from computer science and media studies. The science guys provide the application via which Journalism students can express their thoughts and share rich and useful information with the student population.
