BAKER'S FRIEND

DO NOT REFUSE ORRDERS

student

If you make five cakes in one day then you have a fairly large client base and you should consider yourself professional. Upcoming bakers have fewer orders because they are just getting started which means that refusing an order is not possible. Now why would you refuse an order? If you refuse an order because you are out of ingredients then perhaps you might try ice blocks. The reason why an upcoming baker would turn down an order is because the design is too complicated for them. The other reason is that the design carries an offensive theme involving blood, death, violence or a strong sexual theme. Have you been to a rich funeral where they wear suits and elegant lady outfits with hats and sunglasses and they have baked a death cake for mourners? Usually this cake has a depression emotional disturbing theme including shapes of caskets and creepy scary artifacts. Some bakers are not comfortable with such designs and they say no sorry we cannot do that.
It is not a bakery if it does not bake is a famous quote by Michael. In a competitive environment you cannot afford to be selective. Being selective sunts your progres as a baker and you may be in the same place for a long time. There are certain cakes whose profit margin is huge. The design is complex but the ingredients are affordable. If you refuse this order then you have made a loss. Give the customer what they want and get your pay the theme does not matter. If you feel the cake has an offensive theme do the cake and give the client but do not post the product on social media. Then you have done a business in silence and you have earned your pay.
Refusing an order can also be attributed to what other people might say. If they do not see that design they will not say anything. So from now on refusing orders is not an option.
